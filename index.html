ONLINE INFRINGEMENT DETECTION

We aim to find copyrighted videos online by scraping data and comparing RGB values. If a match of over 70% is found, we label it as potentially infringing. Additionally, we've set up a digital currency system to deter unauthorized uploads to the blockchain, strengthening copyright protection measures.
